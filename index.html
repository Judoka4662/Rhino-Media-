About

  • 2018-2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our journey began as freelancers who decided to pursue their creative work. We didn't have any business experience but as we read many business books, attended many seminars/networking events, and gained clients, we progressed onward.

  • February 13th 2019

    An Agency is Born

    The day our brand became accessible; we finally decided on our name, our design, our logo, and built our website. Even as a real estate advertising agency, freelancers are still the backbone of our company. We are Rhino Media and we will never stop creating your world!
